The deserted village Phia (Aspindza Municipality) is located near Vardzia, 5 km from Khertvisi Castle, 20-22 km from Aspindza to the south, on the right side of the River Mtkvari. The small church of St Theodore is located on the north-west side of the deserted village, on a mountainside. Two inscriptions of the X century were found there in 1949 by G. Gaprindashvili. Altar-stele was embedded in the floor of the altar area, but G Gaprindashvili took it out in the yard of the church to take a photographe.
