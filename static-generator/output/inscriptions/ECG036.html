According to the Corpus of Georgian Inscriptions by Nodar Shoshiashvili the inscription #106. According to its paleographic data, the inscription looks like the other inscriptions of Urbnisi Sioni with: the inscription of Bishop Theodoros and the restorer of Sioni church.
