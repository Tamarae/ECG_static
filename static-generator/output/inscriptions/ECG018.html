Embossed letters are mostly square shaped. The 1st line is on the entire side- facade. The inscription on the top of the arch is eroded. The next lines are interrupted by the arch. The left side of the lines is also eroded. The inscription contains six lines, the first four lines are shaped more freely, and the last two lines are fitted close together. 3 - 2,5 рЃАрЃЏ
