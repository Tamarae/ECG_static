Palestine, Judaean Desert. Italian archeologist Virgilio Corbo discovered the ruins of the Georgian monastery named after St. Theodore near Bethlehem in the Judaean Desert, Palestine in 1952-1953 years. Three Georgian inscriptions were found in the ruins.
